About Dr. Sara Hassan Elfaki

Dr. Sara Hassan Elfaki, MD, FRCOG, FACOG, graduated from Szeged University, Hungary. She holds fellowships from the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (UK) and the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology (USA). Dr. Elfaki also has a postgraduate diploma in Cosmetic Gynecology and a diploma in Obstetric and Gynecology Ultrasound.

With over 20 years of practice, she is highly experienced in managing high-risk pregnancies, handling complications, and performing both emergency and elective gynecological procedures. Dr. Elfaki is also proficient in recent advances in cosmetic gynecology, including the use of energy-based devices, fillers, plasma, and both corrective and cosmetic surgical procedures.
