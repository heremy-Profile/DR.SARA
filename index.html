About Dr. Sara Hassan Elfaki

Education

Dr. Sara Hassan Elfaki, MD, FRCOG, FACOG, graduated from Szeged University, Hungary. She holds fellowships from the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (UK) and the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology (USA).

Experience

With over 20 years of practice, she is highly experienced in managing high-risk pregnancies, handling complications, and performing both emergency and elective gynecological procedures.

Specialties

Dr. Elfaki is proficient in recent advances in cosmetic gynecology, including the use of energy-based devices, fillers, plasma, and both corrective and cosmetic surgical procedures.
